About Howiya

Howiya was born from a completely different idea:

What if clothing could be a way to discover identity… not just fashion?

The journey began in Egypt — a land of civilizations and rich details — where culture blends with history and handcrafted traditions passed down through generations. From the very beginning, the goal was clear: to create a brand that reflects the spirit of communities, carries people's stories, and transforms identity into something that can be worn.

The concept was never about simply drawing inspiration from heritage, but about reintroducing it through a contemporary language that fits today's world. Every piece is the result of deep exploration into culture, symbols, lifestyles, and traditional craftsmanship that holds real human value. We collaborate with artisans, explore manual techniques, and merge them with a modern design vision to create a balance between authenticity and evolution.

Our journey to Europe is not just a geographical expansion, but a step toward sharing Egyptian culture with the world, and redefining fashion as a tool for cultural connection.

We don't just create clothing.

We create a sense of belonging.

We create a story you can wear.
